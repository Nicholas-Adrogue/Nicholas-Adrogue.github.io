About Nico

Hello, my name is Nicholas, but you can call me Nico! I am a second year Applied Mathematics Master's student at Northeastern University in Boston, MA. I graduated from Tufts University in 2023 with a Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering and a minor in Computer Science. I worked for a year as an Environmental Specialist.
